About Me

I am now an associate professor at Zhejiang University. I was a Distinguished Research Fellow in Zhejiang University City College from 2018.10 to 2020.03. I was a Postdoc at Zhejiang University advised by Prof. Li Shanping and a research scientist in Singapore Management University advised by Prof. David Lo.

I got my Ph.D. degree in March 2016 from the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, China. From March 2014 to August 2015, I was a visiting student of Professor Zhenchang Xing in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My research mainly focuses on software engineering, such as developers' behavior analysis, mining software repositories, etc.
