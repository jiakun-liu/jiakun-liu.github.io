About Me

I am currently an associate professor, doctoral supervisor, and Shenzhou Young Scholar at Harbin Institute of Technology. Visit my official homepage.

Prior that, I was a research scientist at Singapore Management University advised by Prof. David LO.

I received my Ph.D. degree in March 2022 from the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, China, advised by Prof. LI Shanping and Dr. XIA Xin.

My research interest is intelligent software engineering. My research involves Artificial Intelligence for Software Engineering (AI4SE) and Software Engineering for Artificial Intelligence (SE4AI). In terms of AI4SE, I mainly use artificial intelligence-related techniques to (1) mine the software repositories and collective intelligence platforms, to understand the behaviors of software practitioners, as well as the software development and evolution processes; and (2) propose intelligent tools to enhance the efficiency of software development and improve the quality and user-experience of software products. In terms of SE4AI, I focus on the software supply chain and software technical debt in AI software.
